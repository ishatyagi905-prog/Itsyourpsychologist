Journaling Prompts

Use these prompts to explore your thoughts and emotions at your own pace.

✏️ What am I feeling right now, and where do I feel it in my body?
✏️ What is one thing I am grateful for today?
✏️ What would I tell my younger self about this situation?
✏️ What drains my energy? What fills it?
✏️ What boundary do I need to set this week?
✏️ What does my ideal day look like — in detail?
🪞

Self-Reflection Worksheet

Gentle questions for weekly self-check-ins. Answer honestly, without judgment.

🌸 How have I grown this week?
🌸 What emotions came up most for me?
🌸 Did I honour my needs and boundaries?
🌸 What do I want to do differently next week?
🌸 Am I being kind to myself?
